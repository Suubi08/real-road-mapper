🌍 Community Impact

Uganda’s roads face persistent challenges due to potholes and poor infrastructure. Our embedded system helps bridge that gap—giving drivers, transporters, and city planners a real-time view of road safety conditions.

🚗 Drivers: Receive timely alerts about road hazards and adjust their routes
🛵 Boda Boda Riders: Navigate dangerous paths more safely and confidently
🏙️ City Planners: Access live pothole data to guide repairs and maintenance
📦 Transport Fleets: Reduce wear-and-tear and improve delivery reliability

This solution turns raw sensor data into civic action—putting people first, through technology.
